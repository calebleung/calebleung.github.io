While I was merely automating my single Steam account, others have told me that they use it on hundreds of accounts, greatly improving their ability to gain Steam trading cards during seasonal sales.

Dive Master

The wiki for Guild Wars 2 is incredibly extensive. Easily accessed from in-game, it is an excellent resource for its players. However, as it is a wiki for all players, none of this information is personalized. This can be an issue for multi-step achievements such as Dive Master.

I created the Dive Master browser extension to alleviate the problem. Using the game's API, I added a personalized column into the Dive Master wiki page which indicates that player's progress with the achievement. Considering that the in-game help for this achievement only reports whether a player has completed "spot #n," this checklist is a vast improvement to a player's experience.

Link Shortner

Have you ever had an idea stuck in your head that wouldn't go away until you executed on it? The Link Shortner project was just that. As the name suggests, it is a link shortner proof-of-concept. Shortlinks for http(s) via GETs and POSTs all while using Google Safe Browsing to safeguard against being used to send users to malicious content.
